Our Journey

Established in 2014, PROJECT CELL is an endeavour to promote innovative thinking amidst young minds. We work towards accomplishing tasks that could be gradually developed on a large scale and solve many social problems that exist today. At Project Cell, we tread towards learning new concepts, techniques and gaining practical experiences on implementation of creative ideas out. The main goal of the Cell is to learn new technologies while developing new projects.

Achievements

Team Project has performed in various national level competations such as E-Yantra (EYIC as well as EYRC), KPIT sparkle and various hackathons held at college level. many projects like Pani Puri Vending Machine, Automatic shopping Robot and Robotic Assistance for TB patient have won an award in E-Yantra Inovation Competation (E-YIC).
